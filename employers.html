Our Recruitment Process

A Structured Hiring Process Designed for Better Results. At Hirovative Solutions, we follow a systematic recruitment process that ensures every candidate is carefully evaluated before being presented to our clients.

01

Requirement Analysis

We discuss your hiring goals, job descriptions, technical skills, experience level, salary range, company culture, and business objectives to develop a customized strategy.

02

Talent Sourcing

We identify the best professionals through premium job portals, LinkedIn, internal databases, referrals, and campus recruitment to source qualified professionals quickly.

03

Candidate Screening

A comprehensive evaluation including resume analysis, technical skill evaluation, HR interviews, communication assessment, and cultural fit analysis.

04

Interview Coordination

We manage the complete interview process, including scheduling, communication, feedback collection, and interview follow-ups for a seamless experience.

05

Offer Management

Our consultants assist in salary negotiations, offer acceptance, joining coordination, and documentation support to ensure a smooth hiring experience.

06

Post Placement Support

Our relationship doesn't end after a candidate joins. We stay connected with both employers and employees to ensure successful onboarding and retention.
